About MOBIQ

A mobile store built on trust, not pressure.

For over a decade, MOBIQ has been the corner of Kochi where families come to buy their first phone, upgrade to the newest flagship, or simply get an honest opinion. Walk in, hold the device, ask anything — no rush, no gimmicks.

  • ✓Every phone is 100% genuine — sealed boxes, official warranty, full bill.
